WHY STAKING?

DimeX staking is designed to strengthen liquidity and support long-term token value.

  • Users stake USDT or DMX
  • Staked assets support liquidity growth
  • Liquidity is added to market pools
  • Strong liquidity supports token stability
  • Users receive staking rewards

This model creates a structured cycle where community participation strengthens liquidity, and liquidity supports token value.

HOW REWARDS WORK

Participants who stake receive rewards in DMX.

  • Held for long-term value
  • Swapped on PancakeSwap
  • Converted to other assets
  • Re-staked for compounding

This flexibility allows users to manage rewards based on their strategy.
